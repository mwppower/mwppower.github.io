About

A Clean Energy consulting company based in India with business offices in Hyderabad and Bangalore. We offer consulting services in the renewable energy sector focussing on Wind and Solar energy areas. MWP Power has over 1250 MW of consulting experience and has mentored a number of successful start-ups.

Our clients include some of the top power corporations in India

Our upcoming project pipeline includes over 1000 MW worth of Wind Energy Licenses in the states of Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and other parts of India.

Our previous experience also includes successfully completed projects in the Biomass, Waste to Energy and Hydro Electricity areas and has helped clients achieve their goals on-time consistently.

Services

Advisory

We provide advice in the sector on business management, operations, research and innovation. We help corporations and start-ups navigate through the industry and provide them with customised expertise and knowledge.

Consulting

We assist in acquiring licenses for Clean Energy projects and help in deal flow sourcing of projects for clients. We undertake professional services in obtaining all the necessary permissions and government approvals required for commissioning the projects.

Mentoring

We help in co-ordination, implementation and execution of Clean Energy projects and planning strategic sales for our clients. We help in establishing a marketplace for projects of varied life cycles.

Our Team

The dynamic management of the company led by our CEO boasts of 25+ years of experience in the clean energy sector. The core team also consists of consultants from London School of Economics (LSE) and Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIM-B). A network of experts, officials and business partners also assist the management team during various phases of a project.
