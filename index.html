What We Do

Advisory

We provide advice in the sector on business management, operations, research and innovation. We help corporations and start-ups navigate through the industry and provide them with customised expertise and knowledge.

Consulting

We assist in acquiring licenses for Clean Energy projects and help in deal flow sourcing of projects for clients. We undertake professional services in obtaining all the necessary permissions and government approvals required for commissioning the projects.
